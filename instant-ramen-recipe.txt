Ingredients:
- Spicy chicken ramen challenge package from grocery store

Directions:
 1. Preheat water in a large pot until boiling.
 2. Add the ramen from package into the water and cook for 5 min.
 3. Add dry vegetables, if any.
 4. Drain water from pot or isolate noodles into bowl and mix spicy flavoring with ramen. 
 5. Enjoy spicy noodle challenge.
